Drop your logo files here:
  - logo.png        (used in nav bar, ~300x72px, transparent background)
  - logo-light.png  (white/light version for the dark footer, same size)
  - favicon.ico     (32x32 browser tab icon)
  - apple-icon.png  (180x180 Apple touch icon)

Then copy favicon.ico and apple-icon.png into src/app/ as well so Next.js
auto-serves them as route-level metadata icons.
